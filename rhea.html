Rhea Raj

Rhea Raj is an Indian-American pop singer-songwriter, producer, and dancer known for her vibrant persona and genre-blending sound that fuses pop, electropop, and dance influences.

She first gained national recognition at just 15 years old on the final season of American Idol in 2016, earning praise from Jennifer Lopez for her voice and stage presence. Trained in music and dance from a young age under her mother's guidance in Indian classical music and Bharatanatyam, Rhea has built a distinctive career as both a performer and songwriter.

Her debut studio album Hunter (2024), followed by Hunter (Tamed Edition), showcased her creative versatility and solidified her place in the modern pop scene. She has been honored with awards such as the Songwriters Hall of Fame x BMI Award (2020) and Spotify x Gold House Artist of the Year (2023).

With hit singles like "Messy," "Hush," "Outside," and "Haute Couture," Rhea has earned acclaim from outlets including Rolling Stone, BBC Radio 1, and Vogue, amassing over 100 million views on TikTok. She has also performed at major festivals such as Coachella, Wango Tango, and Lollapalooza (2025), and is currently headlining her first tour, The Commotion Tour.
